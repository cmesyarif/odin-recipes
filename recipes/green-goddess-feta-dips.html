Green Goddess Feta Dip

Description

This green goddess feta dip, a blend of fresh herbs, avocado, feta, and lime, is very versatile. Serve it with chips or crackers, or use it to dress a grilled steak or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 5 ounces feta cheese
  • 1 avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Add feta, avocado, olive oil, lime juice, parsley, cilantro, green onions, garlic, and black pepper to the bowl of a food processor; blend until smooth. Season to taste with salt.
