Caramel Apple Grapes

Description

Caramel Apple Grapes are a fun, bite-sized treat made by dipping juicy green grapes into caramel and rolling them in crunchy toppings like nuts or cookie crumbs â€” a mini version of the classic caramel apple!

Ingredients

  • 1 (11-ounce) bag caramel bits
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 pound green grapes
  • toppings of choice, such as chopped peanuts, sprinkles, M&M's etc.

Steps

  1. Combine caramel bits and heavy cream in a microwave-saft bowl. Microwave in 30 second intervals until melted, stirring after each interval.
  2. Take a toothpick and stick it into a grape. Dip grape into the melted caramel. Before caramel is completely cooled, roll covered grape in your favorite topping like peanuts or candy sprinkles.
